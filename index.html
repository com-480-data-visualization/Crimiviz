Structural shift

How the pandemic redrew Chicago's crime map

Essay 01 2019 → 2023 9 min read

Lockdowns moved offences off the street and behind closed doors — a displacement that survived reopening. Apartment incidents climbed by two-fifths, sidewalk reports collapsed by a third, and the city's 2024 baseline now sits in a different place than its 2019 one.
